## Key Takeaways

1. **The ecosystem is massive and fast-moving.** Repos are gaining thousands of stars per week. Star counts in this document are approximate snapshots from March 2026.

2. **Three tiers of sophistication exist:**
   - **Unstructured vibe coding**: Just talk to the AI, accept changes, iterate (Karpathy's original vision)
   - **Structured vibe coding**: CLAUDE.md configs, skills, hooks, templates (awesome-claude-code ecosystem)
   - **Methodological frameworks**: BMAD Method, Vibe Ops, Task Master (full agile workflows with AI)

3. **BMAD Method is the leading structured alternative** to pure vibe coding, with ~40k stars and active development.

4. **MCP (Model Context Protocol) servers** are the extensibility layer. The awesome-mcp-servers list at 81k stars shows the ecosystem's depth.

5. **Cross-platform compatibility** is trending. Tools like everything-claude-code support Claude Code, Codex, OpenCode, and Cursor simultaneously.

6. **Official Anthropic resources** (claude-code repo, best practices docs, skills repo, plugins) should be the starting point before exploring community tools.
